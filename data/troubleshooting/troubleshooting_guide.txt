Machine: Roller Mill
Symptom: Excessive vibration
Possible Causes:
- Worn or damaged bearings in roll chocks
- Misalignment of rolls after maintenance
- Loose foundation bolts
- Uneven roll surface due to glazing or scoring
Recommended Actions:
- Inspect and replace worn bearings
- Use dial indicator to check roll alignment; adjust chock position
- Check and torque foundation bolts to spec
- Inspect roll surface; recorrugate or replace if necessary

Machine: Roller Mill
Symptom: Poor grinding efficiency / low extraction
Possible Causes:
- Roll gap set too wide
- Glazed or worn roll corrugations
- Feed rate inconsistency
- Worn feed roller or feed gate misalignment
Recommended Actions:
- Verify and readjust roll gap to product spec
- Check roll surface; recorrugate if glazed
- Check feed gate position and product flow
- Inspect feed roller bearings and replace if worn

Machine: Conveyor
Symptom: Belt slipping or tracking off-centre
Possible Causes:
- Insufficient belt tension
- Worn or seized idler rollers
- Off-centre loading
- Damaged or worn drive pulley lagging
Recommended Actions:
- Adjust take-up tensioner to correct belt tension
- Inspect all idlers; replace seized or damaged units
- Ensure material is loaded centrally on belt
- Inspect drive pulley lagging; replace if worn

Machine: Conveyor
Symptom: Motor overheating
Possible Causes:
- Blocked cooling fins on motor
- Overloaded conveyor beyond motor rating
- Low gearbox oil level causing extra friction
- Belt tension too high creating excessive load
Recommended Actions:
- Clean motor cooling fins and ensure airflow is not blocked
- Check conveyor load and reduce if above rated capacity
- Check and top up gearbox oil to correct level
- Verify belt tension is within specification

Machine: Bucket Elevator
Symptom: Buckets spilling grain at discharge
Possible Causes:
- Head pulley out of alignment
- Damaged or missing buckets
- Belt/chain over-tension causing erratic discharge
- Discharge spout blocked or misaligned
Recommended Actions:
- Check and realign head pulley using laser or straightedge
- Inspect all buckets; replace damaged or missing units
- Verify and adjust chain/belt tension
- Clear and realign discharge spout

Machine: Bucket Elevator
Symptom: Chain or belt stretch causing skipping
Possible Causes:
- Chain wear beyond service limit
- Incorrect chain tension
- Sprocket tooth wear
- Insufficient lubrication
Recommended Actions:
- Measure chain pitch; replace if beyond wear limit
- Adjust drive tension per manufacturer spec
- Inspect drive and tail sprockets; replace if teeth are hooked or worn
- Apply correct lubricant to chain at recommended intervals

Machine: Purifier
Symptom: Sieve blinding / low throughput
Possible Causes:
- Sieve mesh clogged with product
- Worn or stiff brush assemblies not clearing mesh
- Air suction imbalance
- Moisture in product causing sticking
Recommended Actions:
- Remove and clean all sieve frames; check mesh for tears
- Inspect brush condition; replace if brushes are worn flat
- Balance air suction across purifier zones
- Check product moisture; ensure within spec before processing

Machine: Purifier
Symptom: Excessive noise from eccentric drive
Possible Causes:
- Dry or worn eccentric shaft bearings
- Loose guard panels vibrating
- Cracked or loose sieve frame
- Drive belt worn or misaligned
Recommended Actions:
- Lubricate eccentric shaft bearings per schedule
- Inspect and tighten all guard panel fasteners
- Check sieve frames for cracks; replace if necessary
- Inspect drive belt tension and alignment; replace if glazed or cracked
